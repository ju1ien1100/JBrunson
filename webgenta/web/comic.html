C.O.S.M.I.C
Give silent comics a soundtrack — one panel at a time.

An immersive webtoon / manga audio system. Drop in a page and C.O.S.M.I.C uses vision to detect every panel and read its mood & dialogue, then composes a synchronized three-layer soundtrack — background ambience, a melodic motif, and spoken voice-over — that plays as each panel fades in.

Drop a PDF here or click to browse

📄

Pick a single page — panels are detected on that page and the soundtrack is generated panel by panel.

Three layers · three generative models
Background

Stable Audio 3 · Stability AI

Generates the ambient instrumental bed for each panel from a rich text prompt.

Melody

Magenta RT2 · Google

Real-time neural music engine that turns a panel's mood into a melodic motif.

Voice

Suno · voice-over

Voices the dialogue lifted from each panel's speech bubbles in reading order.
